Privacy Policy for Sleep Calculator

Last Updated: [6/27/2025]

1. Data Collection

Sleep Calculator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All sleep calculations are performed locally on your device and are never sent to any servers.

2. Permissions

The App does not require any special permissions as it does not access:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Storage

3. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from this age group.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated "Last Updated" date.
